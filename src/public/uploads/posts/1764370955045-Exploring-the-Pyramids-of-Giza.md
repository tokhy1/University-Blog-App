Egypt is a country full of history and amazing sights. One of the most iconic landmarks is the **Pyramids of Giza**, which attract millions of visitors every year. In this post, we’ll explore the best tips for visiting, what to see, and how to enjoy your trip to the fullest.

## Visiting Tips
- Go early in the morning to avoid crowds.
- Hire a guide to understand the history.
- Don’t forget to bring water and sunscreen.

Traveling in Egypt is an unforgettable experience that everyone should try at least once.
